Welcome to Railway Manager!

With this app you can manage stations, routes and trains efficiently.

🚆 What's this app for?

This app is designed to help users explore and manage railway information efficiently.
Whether you're a train enthusiast, a traveler, or a system administrator, you can access key
data about stations, routes, train models, and active trains.

🔍 What Can I Do With This App?

With this app, you can:
Browse detailed information about train stations and routes ✅
Explore various train models and their specifications ✅
View and manage active trains in the system ✅
If authenticated, add, update, or delete relevant data ✅

🛠️ How Do I Use It?

Using the app is simple:
1️⃣ Navigate through the sections to explore railway data.
2️⃣ Log in or register for additional functionalities like adding or modifying data.
3️⃣ Access key resources through the links below:

🔗 Stations
🔗 Routes
🔗 Train Models
🔗 Trains
